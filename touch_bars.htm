                       Touch Bars


Part number 872 - 2770 Low Floor Rear Door Touch Bar.      
               
Repair parts              
CMBC   Flyer ID Description      
872 - 1215   4, 13, Switch assembly      
872 - 1515     Rubber seal, O ring.      
872 - 1615   6, Trigger, plastic pin.      
872 - 1715   7, Plunger, Aluminum      
               
Repair procedure              
Remove top bracket only. There is no need to remove lower bracket.  
               
Common problem.              
Rubber seal, (O ring), 872 - 1515, wears out causing too much free play.  
When the door closes, touch bar, # 9, activates the switch and reopens the doors.
