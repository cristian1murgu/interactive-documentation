Alternator repair checklist.

  1. Check rotor shaft thread and mounting hole thread for damages. If damages are found, send part(s) to holding area and get new parts.
  2. After dismantling alternator, wash the parts at solvent tank then use washing machine.
  3. After the washing machine, check stator core and rear end body threads for damages. Heli-coil repair is possible for damaged threads.
  4. Visually and by sound check the condition of the rotor .
  5. Check rotor coil and stator coil for open and grounded circuit. Rotor coil current is about 8 amps for 12 and 24 volts alternators.
  6. Check diodes. There are 6 diodes in an alternator.
  7. Use anti seize compound when assemble parts, particularly where blots make contact with aluminum body.
