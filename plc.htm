Low Floor Bus

 

PLC (programmable logic control)

 

Low Floor bus driver’s light part number is 4500034, stamped SL-93 or 93 – 12V15CP.

Do not use other high current draw light bulbs that cause to burn the control unit.

 

Changes in Dip Switch settings will not become effective until the next time the bus is powered up. Reset the power, turning off and then on the battery knife switch, before moving on to the next step, or works.

 

Do not use a test light.

Damage to the I/O modules, input/output cards can occur. The test light is a controlled short and can open traces on the cards and modules.

 

Wire colors for low floor bus.

The following wire colors are used throughout the coach.

 

24 Volt Power             Red-

12 Volt Power              Light blue

PLC input wiring   Yellow

PLC output wiring   Purple

Grounds                       White

 

Output devices

Lights                           Tan

 

Input devices

Switches                       Green

Spare                           Orange

 

Starter automatically cuts out if the engine is cranked for 14 continuous seconds. The starter can be engaged again to crank the engine after a 60 seconds cool-down period.
