                        Orion Fan Heater

Part number 937 - 4240 and 937 - 4241, Orion Highway coach underfloor heater blower motor
937 - 4240 for right hand          
937 - 4241 for left hand          
               
Motor specification              
24 volts,              
27 amps,              
2750 rpm.              
Brushless DC motor.            
               
Parts                
Bearing 936 - 0726 Front and rear bearings      
Fan 937 - 4641            
Controller 937 - 3840 $840, 2005, May.        
               
Procedure                
1 Remove shaft side cover.        
2 Then pull out the armatuer        
Note There is no need to remove rear cover unless there is a problem.
