                                           Kneeling Valve

Part number 800-0770, Kneeling valve
Repair kit 800-0670
Test procedure
1 Supply compressed air, about 60psi ( 25 to 160 psi ) to port "P".
2 Energize solenoid for port "A".
3 Compressed air comes out from port "A".
4 Air flow stops when the solenoid is de-energized.
5 Repeat procedure 1 to 4 for port "B".
Common problems.
Spring inside valve spool is broken and jammed inside
causing too much free play and prevent the valve returns to neutral position
after the solenoid is de-energized.
