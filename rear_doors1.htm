Low Floor bus rear door wiring upgrade.

 

Repair cable kit (87-470-62) is available from New Flyer Industries.

Ctr. No. Part Description Part number
1 Repair cable kit 87-470-62
2 Sensor – Emitter 87-466-70
3 Sensor – Receiver 87-467-70
4 Plastic tube for sensitive edge 87-240-88
5 Plastic tube joiner, connector 87-241-88

Cables are marked SW1 to SW5,

                        SW1 and SW2 are for door switch,

                        SW3 to SW5 are for Photo sensor.

                        SW3 to Brown (power),

                        SW4 to Blue (ground), and

                        SW5 to White (photocell).

 

Cut tie straps and slide plastic tube for sensitive edge through the sheath (cover),
this will make the work easier than inserting cables and plastic tube separately 
through the rear door support.

 

Technicality.

87-466-70, Sensor – Emitter, (Q256 EQ).

Sensor – Emitter sensors have a green LED for dc power “on”, (power on indicator).

Two cables connections are Brown for power and Blue for ground.

 

87-467-70, Sensor – Receiver, (Q25SP6RQ).

Sensor – Receiver sensors have two LEDs, one yellow and one green, a green LED 
for dc power “on” and steady yellow LED means that the sensor is receiving signal 
from Emitter, or the two opposing sensors are aligned and functioning.

Three cables connections are Brown for Power, Blue for Ground, and White for Photocell.
